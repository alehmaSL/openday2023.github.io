To the Bone (2017)

"To the Bone" follows Ellen, a young woman battling anorexia. After numerous failed treatment attempts, she joins an unconventional group home led by an unconventional doctor. The film delves into the complexities of eating disorders, highlighting the importance of self-discovery and support in the journey to recovery. With a raw and compassionate portrayal, "To the Bone" navigates the challenges of mental health with authenticity and empathy

Swallow (2019)

"Swallow (2019)"" delves into the life of Hunter, a pregnant woman grappling with pica disorder. Compelled to ingest perilous objects, her unsettling behavior unravels hidden secrets beneath her facade of marital bliss. This psychological thriller explores themes of control, identity, and societal pressures as Hunter's unconventional compulsion challenges her relationships and self-discovery. The film takes viewers on a gripping journey through the complexities of mental health and the societal expectations imposed upon individuals, creating a thought-provoking and suspenseful narrative.
