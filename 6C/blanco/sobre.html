Propose for school

As a proposal for the school, we thought of making posters and hanging them throughout the school. These posters would contain a message to raise awareness and help people who feel identified and cannot find help. In this way, people will be able to inform themselves and will be able to take this serious problem into account. On the other hand, people who are suffering from this disorder will feel safer
