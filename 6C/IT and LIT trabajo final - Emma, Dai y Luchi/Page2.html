Our Proposal


We’d like to make a group activity for highschool 4th year, 5th and 6th, all separated. We’d like to organize a merienda for each group, to generate a warm environment. Then, a talk shall be given about eating disorders, what types are and signs of those on oneself or others. After these, every student shall write on paper, anonymously, something about their relationship with food.



If anyone feels uncomfortable, they should do a drawing that shows body acceptance and healthy relationship with food, or simply hand in blank.These papers will be put on a box, and then they would be read out loud, so people will notice how many others go through the same, even if they can´t know who is it.
