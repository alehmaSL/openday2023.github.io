Bulimia Nervosa In Argentina

In our country, about 15% of teenagers suffer from bulimia nervosa. According to studies, we are the second country with the highest number of cases in the whole world. Stereotypes of what people believe are perfect body types influence many people’s self confidence and affect how they feel about themselves.

In our school, even if it’s not talked about, many students must feel unhappy with themselves, insecure. We believe if we raise awareness about this, perhaps some could feel less lonely, and know it’s not wrong to be different, as we all are.
