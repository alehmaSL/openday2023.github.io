Understanding race inequality
Action at our school

We invite every member of our school community to share their own experiences with race inequality. Your voice matters, and your story can help shed light on the complexities of this issue. By coming together and engaging in open discussions, we can create a more inclusive and equitable school environment where everyone feels valued and heard.
To share your experiences, please consider participating in upcoming focus groups or submitting anonymous stories through our dedicated portal. Let us stand united in our commitment to combating race inequality and building a school community that celebrates diversity, promotes understanding, and nurtures an environment of respect and inclusion for all.
