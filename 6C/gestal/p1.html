Plan of Action

Our global action to help create awareness about eating disorders is hosting a no judgment art and writing expositions. Through the various art classes and workshops, students will be exposed to the subject and will be able to create art about their experiences. Those who don’t want to be a part are not going to be forced, but those who want to participate but aren’t part of the classes are invited to take part in the display.

Things like body positivity, the struggle with our image, dealing with an eating disorder, knowing someone who is struggling and wanting to help, among others.

This project will boost creativity among students and will give them a space to express themselves and their issues. It will generate a safe space and create connections.
