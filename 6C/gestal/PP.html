The problem of eating disorders in teens is an issue that has plagued society for a long time. It is difficult to tackle, since it may not be apparent who can be affected by this issues and even harder to help them confront them. The official definition by the American Psychiatrist Association for eating disorders are “behavioral conditions characterized by severe and persistent disturbance in eating behaviors and associated distressing thoughts and emotions. They can be very serious conditions affecting physical, psychological and social function.

Eating disorders are not something that is talked about enough in education. Many don’t know its extent or even the basic information about it.

Eating disorders can affect all types of people. They’re complex issues that affect not only the physical aspect of a person but the psychological as well. The most common types of eating disorders are Anorexia, Bulimia, and Binge Eating Disorder (BED). These are not the only ones and are a few among many others that affect thousands.
